Overview

Purpose

The Kampala Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity

Audience

We are aiming for a wide range of folks who want an wholesome exprience in Commerce and business Development. We offer best services to a diverse majority of client. Our audience will include, but not limited to; small businesses, upcoming enterprenuers, individuals & groups.
